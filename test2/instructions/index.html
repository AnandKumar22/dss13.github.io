Profile :

  1. You can change all your details here except username and mail id.

  2. Click on the tab which you want to change and type to change it.

  3. Click on the Update button in the bottom to update your details.


Manage Teams :

  • Event Registrations:

    1. You can register for any event here.
    2. You can select any event in the drop box which is shown genre wise.
    3. Click on the event which you want to register and then click on the register button.
    4. The events which you have registered already will be shown in green colour.

  • Team Formation :

    1. You can form a team in your desired event.
    2. Select the event and then then click on create team button.
    3. A team will be automatically formed in the Team Management tab with a specific team id.
    4. Only the event in which you have registered will be shown in the drop box.

  • Team Management :

    1. Only the person who creates the team can manage the team. He would be the admin of the team.
    2. Admin can send invitations to any other user by searching their name or username in the search box.
    3. Once the user accepts the invitation, his name would appear in the team membership tab.
    4. Admin can also delete his team by clicking on Dismantle Team button.
    5. He can also see the no of members in his team in his tab.

  • Team Membership :

    1. In this tab, you can see which teams you are a part but not the admin of.
    2. You can chose to leave the team of you wish to here.

Manage Invitations :

  • Pending Requests:

    1. In this tab, you can see the requests which you got to join the teams for a specific event.
    2. You can accept the invitation or ignore the invitation.

  • Invites Sent :

    1. This tab contains invitations which you have sent to other members to join your team.

Submissions :

  1. You can select the event in which you want to make the submissions and then upload your submission.
  2. Submission must be in .pdf, .docx and .zip only.
  3. The maximum size of submission is 5 Mb.
  4. You can also make the submission more than once.
