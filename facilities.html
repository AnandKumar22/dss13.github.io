    Xerox Shop

      9:00 am to 10:00 pm
      Near B Block Ground Floor

    The newly opened Xerox Shop in LBS Hall of Residence caters to the everyday needs of the Shastrians. Be it printing the assignments of the procrastinating ones or looking after the daily needs of the regular muggus, Raju da makes sure that one does not leave unhappy. We sure can say that our xerox shop caters to the soul of our academics.


  • 02

    Gym

      6 am to 9 am & 5:30 pm to 8 pm
      B Block Ground Floor

    The gym in LBS Hall of Residence is often seen bustling with activity. After a weary long day, full with class and labs and whatnot, Shastrians often find solace in this very place because what's a better stress reliever than a refreshing workout? The gym is fully eqipped with instruments that are needed for a satisfying workout needed to siphon of the weariness of everyday life.


  • 03

    Library

      Friday and Saturday, 9 pm to 10 pm
      AB-301

    The hall library is equipped with abundant books covering a plethora of topics and subjects. Shastrians can issue course textbooks and read everyday periodicals. The hall library is often seen with students coming over to the discuss the projects and stuff panning over a range of fields.


  • 04

    Milkstall

      7:00 pm to 2:00 am
      C Block 1st Floor

    The milkstall in 1st Floor C Block is one place which has formed a niche of itself in the heart of every Shastrian. Be it veg maggi, sandwiches, egg-omelettes and flavoured milk ranging from everday Bournvita to lip licking keshar badam, this milkstall serves it all. The milkstall is often a night-time hunger relief to many Shastrians and becomes a place for a quick bite and a quick gossip between friends.


  • 05

    Nescafe and Juice Center

      11:00 am to 2:00 am
      Near LBS Hall Main Gate

    Nescafe is often seen bustling with people willing to get some jucies flowing in their body (figuratively or otherwise). Nescafe often serves as the place where the caffine lovers come to get their shot. It is also a place for people who are bored of the bland mess food and a quick bite of the well cherished Masala Patties is a little celebration in itself.
